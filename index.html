Crystal Collector

A random number will be chosen at the beginning of each game. Your goal is to match the number by clicking on the 4 crystals below.
Each crystal is worth a specific number of points. Clicking on a crystal will add points to your total.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score surpasses the random number.
The value of each crystal is a mystery until you click on it.
At the start of every new game, the value of the crystals will change.
Good Luck!
